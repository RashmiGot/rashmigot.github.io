Research

Harnessing the power of the DJA: extreme red sources with JWST/NIRSpec

Current PhD project, using all public JWST/NIRCam and JWST/NIRSpec data stored on the Dawn JWST Archive (DJA) to identify extreme red galaxies in the high-redshift universe.

Stellar mass functions of optically-faint galaxies with JWST

Master's thesis work. Paper published in MNRAS: https://doi.org/10.1093/mnras/stae754

Infrared-radio correlation of star-forming galaxies in COSMOS-XS

Leiden LEAPS programme. Manuscript in preparation.

DSFGs and their contribution to the obscured SFRD

Master's research project using FIR data and SED-fitting with MAGPHYS to determine the contribution of dusty star-forming galaxies (DSFGs) to the cosmic star-formation rate density (SFRD).
